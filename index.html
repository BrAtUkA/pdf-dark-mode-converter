What Is PDF Dark Mode Conversion?

PDF dark mode conversion transforms standard bright-white PDF documents into eye-friendly dark-background versions you can save and keep. Unlike browser extensions that only tint your screen, this tool generates a real PDF file with your chosen color scheme baked in - open it on any device, any reader, and it stays dark. The converter runs entirely in your browser: your files are never uploaded anywhere, and it even works offline after the first visit.
